Drunken Master Musashi

is not to be mistaken for an old, senile drunkard, even if he stashes the most potent of alcholic beverages, throughout his living quarters, that are located in the towering apartment complex, full of nicely laid out corridors and an Eastern influenced vibe, running from one paper lantern to the next. You see, he is retired and a well off businessman. He took to martial arts as he was very young and has carved a respectful carreer among the elite, not excluding the mafia and some of their more notorious, yet fine gentlemen, on the Eastern hemisphere - all of whom Musashi is able to carry out business deals with, when it comes to dealing in money, men and their powerful arsenal of hard hitters, of the human variety - walking tanks, with fingertips sharp as any tanto. Steer away from Musashi, for now, for he is hard to reach and could get get under your skin, before he even gets a punch in, as his style dictates -- we'll get back to him throughout our carreers, on his latest acquisitions, especially his shadiest and most high traffic dojo. Know that he once trained under Shaolin monks, for the good part of a decade, before emerging with a sudden hankering to take on his own Snake Style disciples, that he believes are the most powerful and flexible practicioners, when coupled with unseen cybernetic implants, and when trained by neurally enhanced street fighters.
