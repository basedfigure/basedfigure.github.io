Everything beneath here is worked on either sporadically or consistently, but only between certain time spans during the year, and they are laid out so as to follow Parkinson's Law, in that no single project should be made to take too much focus or energy all at once. The reason for laying out the groundwork is solely for seeing through the mental fog, tunnel vision and bad practices--to not regret later inactions, by leaving something else untouched.

killh:
Killhouse is a pen & paper roleplaying game, to be written, with secret societies and vampire houses of gothic lineage. The git should eventually contain scans of my handwritten design papers, doodles, floor plans, the final book in PDF format, and bonus footage of my workspace for writing and playing--with attention to detail in establishing the proper atmospherics for a play. The die system is a D6-based one--with no miniatures, nor heavy tactical play involved anywhere along the way. D6 games are relatively rare for the kinds of games that I want to play, so that is my initial motivation in writing mine.
