BioGriever: A Dark Future (RPG)

BioGriever's first edition is a 2D6 based roleplaying game, with an attitude! Nothing else is required for play besides these rules along with pen n' paper, six-sided dice -- or skip all that and use only the EmulaTotM: gamemaster emulator (with more on each later).

1.0 - Theater of the Mind (TotM)

Immersing your mindscape into that of a biomodded street warrior -- or other alternate figures in this hard sci-fi setting -- is a rewarding practice, and the provided materials are designed to ease your transition to an imagined setting, full of fun scenarios, that no other form of play can offer in the same fashion. The game's rules are the sauce of the dish; the writing and immersion -- the meat, but maybe we're different you and I, and you'll be the one to flip the script. Slight tacticality and crunch will be introduced as the game progresses, but not ever at the expense of snapping you out of your immersive state just for the sake of doing math.

2.0 - Combat!

Lead, smoke, homing missiles and other deadly rounds are a commodity in a Dark Future setting -- and more often than not, you're going to be either sending 'em or catching some. So make sure to keep your piece at your side and wear a vest, as you find yourself catching heat.
